Q: Do you Provide Franchise of these brands?

Yes, The Rolling Plate provides franchise privileges to these brands. One brand at one location is called 1 Unit and, 1 unit Franchise cost starts from 2.90 Lakh rupees.

Q: What is the procedure to take the Franchise?

1. Choose the Brand and location.

2. Call/WhatsApp to our brands

3. Pay - Online / Cheque / DD

4. Sign the Agreement within 24 - 48 hours.

Q: What is the cost of Franchise?

The cost to buy an Unit Franchise starts from 2, 90,000 + 18% GST (Two lakhs ninety thousand only + 18% GST) + Marketing charges of 50K for first year.

Q: Is marketing mandatory?

Yes , As the competition is higher in Cloud kitchen Space , The Marketing (Zomato/Swiggy) is important to get the visibility of the restaurant .

Q: How the Franchise Operates?

This is a FOCO Model franchise kitchen i.e. Franchise-Owned Company-Operated. From setting up the kitchen to hiring of the staff and buying equipment’s, everything is done by the company which also manages the monthly expenses of the kitchen. This includes paying rentals, salaries, and all other expenses.

Q: What is the profit margin of the Franchise?

The franchise gets 18% of the GROSS SALES of the business done by the Brand taken by the franchise.

Q: What are the monthly expenses of the Franchise?

There are NO MONTHLY EXPENSES other than marketing and promotional requirements as needed at the investor’s discretion.

Q: What is the term period of the agreement of Franchise?

The term period of franchise is of 3 years. The franchise can be renewed for the next 3 years after paying 30% of the franchise fee at the time of renewal.

Q: Where are the locations, can I take a Franchise?

We are available in Delhi NCR. But since this is a FOCO Model so franchise have no involvements in the operations so anybody from anywhere in the world can take the franchise in Delhi NCR.

Q: What is the ROI?

10 – 18 Months approx. ( Depends on the Marketing & Positioning )

Q: Can I operate the franchise from different states or different country?

Yes, in this model there is no investor-side involvement in the operations. So you can monitor it from different states or from the different countries also.

Q: How will I know the sales of My Brand?

Since this is an online restaurant, the sales are via online delivery portals like Zomato, Swiggy and other delivery partners. We will give the admin Id & Password of the platform to the franchise to keep a check.

Q: How do I sign the agreement if I am not from Delhi?

The agreement can be singed online. We will send the agreement online and you can scan & print the agreement & sign it.

Q: Do I need to have a GST Number or a company to take the franchise?

No, you can take the franchise on the individual Name.

Q: Is the any experience required of food business or any business?

No

Q: How much time it takes after paying to start the business?

The maximum time taken for the integration is 45 – 60 days.

Q: Can I and my friend start 1 single franchise together?

Yes

Q: I want to start in my city / state, how can I do that?

We can collaborate on Master Franchise model. Mail us for the further details for Master Franchise Model.

Q: What are the sales of Existing Clients?

The income depends on the marketing of the unit which is the core responsibility of the unit Franchise. Generally a unit does a revenue of 30k*- 1Lac* Rs. by end of 1st year.

Q: What if I want to exit?

The resturant franchise cannot be returned but it can be transferred.
