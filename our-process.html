Our Process

A true blue first in the Indian Food & Beverage Industry, The Rolling Plate brings a new dimension to the Online Food Franchise industry. Combining exceptional business planning, modeling, and execution with a remarkably unique blend of ecclesiastic cuisines.

Starting out as a brainstorm idea in 2019, the company materialized because of the sheer potential of the plan. Growth and developmental opportunities popped up fast and soon we were well over half a dozen franchises in. Our exemplary revenue-generating model and the whopping profit from them brought Cloud kitchens to a truly national level.

India being a diverse palette of culinary traditions, fusions, and sheer delights, we thought only an equally diverse and impeccable venture could do justice to it. We bring along with us our multiple brands serving food from all corners of the country and even international imports like Chinese and continental. We thrive on innovation and constantly expand our horizons on cuisines. We raise the bar, raise the revenue and raise the roof to make our big splash in the industry!
